💫 Aaj woh din hai jis din meri zindagi ne pehli saans li thi... Unki pehli aahat, unka pehla ehsaas, unki pehli khushboo – sab kuch aaj tha 💖 Mera baccha aaj jaake usi saal ki ho gayi, jo hum dono ke liye sabse khaas hai — 22, jab hum ek hue the 🌸 Kya doon aapko is din? Sab kuch toh le gayi ho aap mera...

🫀 Aap woh ho jisse main sab kuch haar betha... Meri khushi aap ho, mera sukoon aap, mera chain aap, meri zindagi aap, meri duniya aap ho 💕 Mere is duniya mein aane ki wajah bhi aap. Agar aap nahi hote toh kuch bhi nahi hota... Aap kya nahi ho? Sab kuch toh ho mere liye

Dekh toh nahi sakta aapko aaj... mehsoos bhi nahi kar sakta, maya door ki baat, Isliye in chaar dinon se, bina khaye, bina piye,bina nhaaye,mere ji ke periods mey tak bina rahe har pal har ghadi sirf isi mein ghoomta raha... Sirf itna chahta tha ki chahe jitni bhi takleef ho, thakaan ho, chahe mujhe aap se door hi kyu na rehna pade... Bas itna chaha ki jab bhi aap ye dekhein, aapke chehre pe muskaan aajaye... Ye Dekhke agar aap khush ho jaayein, aake gale lag lein mujhe 💞 toh mere liye sabuch hoga

🎁 Par waada hai mera — aane wale waqt mein aapke is har din ko itna khaas bana dunga ... Ki aapko khud samajh na aaye, kaunsa sabse behtar tha, kaunsa sabse zyada accha 💫
